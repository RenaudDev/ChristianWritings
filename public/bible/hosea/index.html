Book Of Hosea

Book of Hosea, also spelled Osee, the first of 12 Old Testament books that bear the names of the Minor Prophets , considered as one book, The Twelve, in the Jewish canon. According to the superscription, Hosea began his prophetic activity during the reign of Jeroboam II (c. 786–746 BC). His prophetic announcements indicate that he was active until near the fall (721 BC) of the northern kingdom of Israel, the scene of his entire ministry. [ Source ]
